Who are we?

The C.A.F.E Foundation is a non-profit association that unites friends, professionals, volunteers and the aid of public and private institutions to provide help to children and elderly people with an ambient of faith and love.

Ethics

Our personal conduct consists in act properly.

Honesty

Integrity, sincerity and transparency is our way to be, feel and act till accomplish the harmony between what we say and do.

Solidarity

Identification, empathy and cooperation with our colleagues, the community and society in general.

Responsibility

Making possible our compromise to work and help people.

  • Vision
  • Mission

Vision

Strengthen our mission with the union, talent and creativity of more friends, professionals, volunteers, public and private institutions, with their economic and intellectual support we’ll be able to make the lives of tons of people better.

Misión

Build a better quality of life for the people through aid programs to bring support, tools and resources for economic, social and self development.

Objetivo

Join forces in benefit of all who needs help in order to give them better quality of life through economic, educative, recreative help thanks to a multidisciplinary team able to make contributions to their development from an ambient of love and faith.

What we do

Visit to the orphanage

Our childs are the seeds of the future, giving them our support is the hope for a society filled with love and faith.

Visit to the nursing home

Elderly people are like children, they need love and care, Our support is the hope for a better quality of life in an environment of faith and love.

Events

The events made by the foundation are the point of encounter for wellbeing, enrichment, quality of life, faith and love.

Empowerment of the women

The biggest social commitment is with the family and women are who represent the bases of a home, always giving a sensation of wellbeing for all the members of the family building an environment of faith and love.

Sport Programs

Sport is a set of disciplines that we develop according to the category with the end of motivating a life in movement and a wholesome body, mind and spirit.

Social Aids

In search of a better life, we encounter sometimes obstacles in our way we cannot go through without aid, we want to help people when they go through these obstacles giving benefits and compensations to anyone who needs them. Many are the shortcomings were we’re going to plant seeds that will grow and bear fruit.
